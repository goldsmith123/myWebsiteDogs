Psychology --------- The Ten Commandments

Roulette can be fun and for the very few, profitable. Sticking to a few simple rules in this action packed fast game can make the difference between success or failure.

1. Understand the game you are playing.

2. Always have a detailed schedule in mind.

3. Have a detailed strategy.

4. Never have any more money with you than you can afford to lose. Don't give yourself access to more money.

5. Stay focused.

6. Treat yourself if you leave a winner.

6. If you lose, be honest. Don't cover up your losses. Everyone loses something sometimes.

7. Remember; A fool and his money are easily parted.

8. Don't feel guilty. It will only lead to self-punishment.

9. Don't blame others for your gambling.

10. Be happy. Enjoy yourself.
